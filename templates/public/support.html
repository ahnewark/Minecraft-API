I can't log in to my account

In case you are seeing the error message: "Login throttled, too many attempts. Try again in a couple of minutes.", this is a security measure to keep hackers from guessing passwords. If this happens to you, just wait for some minutes and then re-enter your account information (this might be caused by you sitting in school or at work).

Everything regarding premium accounts




What is a premium account?

A premium account is what you get when you purchase the game on our purchase page. First and foremost you get access to the full game as well as every future version until the release. A lot of features like the survival mode, crafting, mobs and the inventory are only playable in the premium version. Unlike the classic version, you can now connect to Minecraft servers all around the world and enjoy the game with other players as well as use custom skins to individualize your character.

Everything related to payments




Everything related to the actual transaction of the money






My transaction was successful. But where is my game?

The game is only sold as a digital copy, so you won't receive any sort of physical components like a CD or a box.

Once the transaction was successfull, you will receive a confirmation email. Due to the big attention Minecraft is getting at the moment the server at times needs a little time to send out the emails. In that case you can try to log in to the game without having received a confirmation email.
In any case we recommend you not to retry purchasing the game over and over again, because eventually the purchases will go through and even though Minecraft is superb, at some point you CAN end up having more Minecraft than you want.
My transaction request was cancelled

This is usually a case for the credit card company/PayPal. For the transaction to be successful, make sure that there is enough money on the account and that all the information there is correct. The transaction usually takes 1-4 working days. In case you are not paying in euro, US dollars or pounds sterling additional fees may be applied by your card provider/PayPal.

My credit card gets rejected

This happens when you are using a credit card which isn't supported by our system. Usually you get an error message saying that the credit card "is ineligible for 3-D Secure payments". Until further credit cards are supported, you have to switch to another credit card institute or use PayPal for purchasing Minecraft.
