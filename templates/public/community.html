Community

Here are some nice active community resources for discussing Minecraft. Please note that all of these are third-party and aren't hosted by us. We link them because they're great.

Official Resources

  • The Word of Notch - The one and only blog for Minecraft fans
  • @Notch - Follow notch's ramblings on twitter
  • Facebook - Join our community on Facebook
  • Get satisfaction - Give some feedback, make your voice heard.
  • Minecraft forums - Official forums, Notch reads these
  • Minecraft wiki - Comprehensive community run Wiki

IRC

esper.net and other IRC networks aren't affiliated with Minecraft or Mojang, so their staff can't help with support for our game. Ask inside the channels (preferably #MineCraftHelp) for that.

  • #minecraft on irc.esper.net - Official IRC channel, run by Notch. (channel rules)
  • #MineCraftHelp on irc.esper.net - Help and support from community members.
  • #mcchatter on irc.esper.net - For general community chatter. (channel rules)

Forums and Communities

  • MojangSupport - Unofficial support by Tom Costello
  • Minecraftopia - Great (and good looking!) resource
  • Minecraft Community on Steam - Over 1000 members!
  • Minecraft on Reddit
  • CraftHub
  • World of Minecraft
  • Minecraft on Mod DB
  • Minecrafting.ru - A Russian forum
  • Minecraft forum - A Spanish forum

  • Mojang Community - A fan-made community for all Mojang's games
  • Minecraft.fr - A French Minecraft site, potato cubes!
  • Minecraftfr.com - Another French Minecraft community.

Indie Game Development

Want to make your own game? Want to see what games other people are making?

  • TIGSource - Lots of talent! Minecraft originated here.
  • JavaGaming.org - Other java game developers. Neglected after Oracle took over.
  • Indie Games - The blog is awesome, and so is the podcast.
  • Ludum Dare - A 48 hour game development competition.
